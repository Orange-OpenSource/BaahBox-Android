Models a bundle of balloon game configuration elements. These values defines steps of progression : - minThreshold1;maxThreshold1 is the first step - minThreshold2;maxThreshold2 is the second step - minThreshold3;maxThreshold3 is the third step, - [ minThreshold4 ; maxThreshold4[ is the fourth step, the final before the congratulations!

Parameters

minThreshold1 - The minimal value of the 1st step of progression

maxThreshold1 - The maximal value of the 1st step of progression

minThreshold2 - The minimal value of the 2nd step of progression

maxThreshold2 - The maximal value of the 2nd step of progression

minThreshold3 - The minimal value of the 3rd step of progression

maxThreshold3 - The maximal value of the 3rd step of progression

minThreshold4 - The minimal value of the 4th step of progression

maxThreshold4 - The maximal value of the 4th step of progression
