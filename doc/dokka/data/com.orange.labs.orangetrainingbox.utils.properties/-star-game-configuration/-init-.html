(JVM) StarGameConfiguration(minThreshold1: Int, maxThreshold1: Int, minThreshold2: Int, maxThreshold2: Int, minThreshold3: Int, maxThreshold3: Int)

Models a bundle of star game configuration elements. These values defines steps of progression : - minThreshold1;maxThreshold1 is the first step - minThreshold2;maxThreshold2 is the second step - [ minThreshold3 ; maxThreshold3[ is the third step, the final before the congratulations!

Parameters

minThreshold1 - The minimal value of the 1st step of progression

maxThreshold1 - The maximal value of the 1st step of progression

minThreshold2 - The minimal value of the 2nd step of progression

maxThreshold2 - The maximal value of the 2nd step of progression

minThreshold3 - The minimal value of the 3rd step of progression

maxThreshold3 - The maximal value of the 3rd step of progression
